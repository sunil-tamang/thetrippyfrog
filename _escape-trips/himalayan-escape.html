Himalayan Escape




 Overview

The HIMALAYAN ESCAPE TRIP is almost a week-long trip of 4 Days and 3 Nights. This trip is meant for escaping from the stress and filling the soul and body with new positive energy. Enjoy it while you can, and be on this trip, create new "Trippy" memories and be positive, refreshed and again.

MANALI

SOLANG VALLEY
KASOL

 Escape Journey

  • DAY 0 : DELHI-MANALI BY A/C VOLVO BUS

    Arrive Delhi Volvo Bus stand, Board the Volvo bus at 05 PM and start your ever memorable Manali tour package . Overnight travel by Volvo Bus.

  • DAY 01 : MANALI LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

    Morning arrive at Manali and transfer to hotel After Fresh up at Manali hotel , depart for half day local sightseeing tour covering places like Hadimba Devi temple, Manu temple, Vashisht Bath, Van Vihar and evening shopping at the local market ( Mall ). Overnight stay & dinner at Manali hotel.

  • DAY 02 : SNOW POINT (FULL DAY)

    Places:
    1. Nehru kind
    2. Gulaba
    3. Rahla waterfall
    4. Madi
    5. Rahri naala
    Today depart for some wandering and for sight seeing which will be done for available snow points like Gulaba , Kothi or Madi or Solang valley. Solang valley is heaven for adventure lovers, and paragliding . zorbing, skiing can be done on extra cost. ( Subject to availability). Return back to hotel , dinner & overnight stay at Manali hotel.

  • DAY 03 : KULLU VALLEY-MANIKARAN (FULL DAY)

    Places / Activities :
    1. River Rafting
    2. Vaishnu Devi Temple
    3. Famous Kullu Shawl factory
    4. Manikaran hotspring

    After breakfast, prepare for whole day sightseeing tour to Kullu Valley, which includes - Vaishno Devi Temple, Kullu Shawl industry, Kasol Valley, Manikaran Shiv Temple and Gurudwara. Overnight stay at hotel in Manali.

  • DAY 04 : NAGAR + MANALI = DEPARTURE

    Places:
    1. Naggar castle
    2. Roerich Art Gallery Museum
    3. Jana waterfall
    4. Checkout

    In the morning, we will take you to the Naggar Castle of Kullu valley. Not much distance from there, is the Roerich Art Gallery Museum, where you can have the view of the whole of Himachal Pradesh in the paintings of the famous Russian painter, writer, archaeologist, theosophist - Nicholas Roerich. In the evening, iff the time left, you can explore the Shopping malls of Manali on your own. In the evening at 06 PM, board the Volvo AC bus to Delhi for overnight journey.

  • MORNING ARRIVAL AT DELHI

    You will reach at Delhi around 08-09 AM.

  • END OF TOUR!
